Our Journey

"And I tell you that you are Peter and on this rock I will build my church, and the gates of Hades will not overcome it."

Matthew 16:18

At the beginning of year 2005 we began a small group prayer in home, and it made the pathway to our present Shalom church. During our prayers a sister who lived in the home had special prayer request regarding a church for us. God has kept this burden in her heart. As we prayed for it God answered our prayers and gave us a church in Milano city. Throughout these years God has done great and mighty things in our church.

"The Lord's hand was with them, and a great number of people believed and turned to the Lord."

Acts 11:21

All glory, honor and praise be to our God forever!
