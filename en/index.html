Our Journey

The beginnings of Shalom Gospel Church trace back to early 2005, starting as a small prayer group in a family home.

During those prayers, a sister had a special request: that God would grant us a church of our own. God heard and answered our prayers, and on March 6, 2005, He established the "Shalom" church in the city of Milan.

Since then, God has led us through a wonderful growth. Today, we primarily serve the Sri Lankan community in Italy across Milan and Verona, sharing the love and message of Jesus Christ.

All glory, honor, and praise be to our Creator forever!
